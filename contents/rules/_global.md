# Global Rules

whisperwall is an anonymous imageboard for loose conversation, small projects, questions,
media, and board culture. Keep the place usable for other people.

## Basic Conduct

- Do not post illegal content, request illegal content, or link to it.
- Do not threaten, harass, stalk, dox, impersonate, or coordinate harm against anyone.
- Do not spam, flood, evade moderation, or automate posting without permission.
- Do not post malware, credential phishing, deceptive downloads, or shock content.
- Do not derail threads with repeated off-topic arguments once asked to stop.

## Posting

- Stay roughly on topic for the board and thread.
- Mark spoilers and sensitive material plainly in the post body.
- Use a clear subject when creating a thread.
- Do not repost deleted material to challenge a moderation decision.
- Keep uploads reasonably small and relevant to the conversation.

## Images And Media

- You are responsible for the files and links you post.
- Do not upload copyrighted material unless you have the right to share it or your use is legally permitted.
- Do not upload private images, revenge material, exploitative content, or content involving minors in a sexual context.
- Staff may remove media without notice if it creates legal, safety, or operational risk.

## Identity

- Anonymous posting is allowed.
- Tripcodes are optional and only prove that the same password was used again.
- Capcodes are staff-only. A typed name that says "Mod" or "Admin" does not grant staff status.
- Do not attempt to pass yourself off as staff.

## Moderation

- Staff may delete, archive, pin, ban, or lock content to keep the site functional.
- Moderation is not a debate stage. Good-faith questions are fine; evasion and harassment are not.
- These rules are not exhaustive. If something is clearly abusive or risky, staff may act on it.

## Legal

Posts are user-submitted third-party content. Whisperwall does not endorse user posts and is
not responsible for third-party claims, copyright ownership, or licenses attached to content
submitted by users.
