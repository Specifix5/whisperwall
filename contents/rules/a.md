# /a/ Rules

/a/ is for anime, manga, light novels, visual novels, adaptations, studios, artists, and related ^\_^
fan culture.

## On Topic

- Episode threads, chapter discussion, recommendations, production talk, scans of official previews, and collection posts are welcome.
- Mark spoilers for recent episodes, chapters, endings, and major twists.
- Fan art discussion is fine, but credit the artist when you know the source.

## Not Allowed

- Piracy links, full chapter dumps, full episode uploads, or requests for illegal streams.
- Sexual content involving minors or characters presented as minors (including but not limited to: loli/shota content).
- Harassment of artists, translators, fans, studios, or VAs.
- Unmarked spoilers in general threads.

## Good Threads

- Put the series title in the subject.
- Say whether you want spoilers, spoiler-free discussion, or recommendations.
- For recommendations, list a few things you already liked.
