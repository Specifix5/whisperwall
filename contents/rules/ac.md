# /ac/ Rules

/ac/ is for academia, study, research habits, papers, classes, writing, and learning.

## On Topic

- Study advice, research methods, paper discussion, academic life, writing help, and reading groups are welcome.
- Homework help is allowed when you show your own attempt and ask a specific question.
- Critique of ideas is fine; personal attacks on students, teachers, or researchers are not.

## Not Allowed

- Asking others to complete assignments, exams, applications, or graded work for you.
- Plagiarism, academic fraud, fake citations, or bypassing proctoring and course systems.
- Posting private course material, paid PDFs, answer keys, or personal student information.
- Medical, legal, or financial advice presented as professional guidance.

## Good Threads

- Include the field, level, and what you have read already.
- Quote short passages only when needed and cite the source.
- Separate speculation from evidence.
