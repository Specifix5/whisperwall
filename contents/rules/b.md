# /b/ Rules

/b/ is the loose random board. It can be messy, casual, and strange, but it still has to be
usable.

## On Topic

- General chat, jokes, edits, desktop decoration, image finds, and passing thoughts are welcome.
- Short-lived threads are fine.
- Threads do not need to be serious, but they should still have some visible intent.

## Not Allowed

- Raids, harassment campaigns, doxing, impersonation, or targeted abuse.
- Spam floods, repeated bait, or posting the same image/thread over and over.
- Illegal content, exploitative content, malware, phishing, or shock material.
- Content whose main purpose is to make the board unreadable.

## Good Threads

- Give people something to reply to.
- Keep image dumps themed enough that they are not just noise.
- Take personal fights elsewhere before they become the thread.
