# /mu/ Rules

/mu/ is for music discussion: albums, songs, scenes, gear, production, shows, collecting, and
listening threads.

## On Topic

- Recommendations, reviews, listening logs, charts, instruments, production, and music history are welcome.
- Link official pages, Bandcamp, streaming pages, label pages, or artist pages when useful.
- Sharing your own music is fine when you are clear that it is yours.

## Not Allowed

- Piracy requests, leaked albums, private download links, or instructions for bypassing paywalls.
- Harassing artists, fans, or small scenes.
- Low-effort genre wars that only repeat insults.
- Posting copyrighted files as uploads when you do not have the right to share them.

## Good Threads

- Mention genre, era, region, or similar artists when asking for recommendations.
- Explain what you liked or disliked instead of only posting a score.
- For production help, include your DAW, plugin chain, and what you are trying to fix.
